About

Cyber laypersons seem to be unaware, or they do not know about that there are a large number of cyber-attacks that happen on every day. Interest in cyber security issues often focuses on incidents and how to deal with them after it had happened. While a concern for prevention in better cybersecurity have fallen behind. This website is developed for the general internet users to access and increase their level of cybersecurity awareness.

This website serves as a tool to help the cyber laypersons since they are more likely to fall victims to cyber-threats than technology savvy persons. This website is developed to help them improve their awareness of cybersecurity.
