Cybersecurity Awareness: A Digital Media Approach

The development of information and communication technology is growing at a fast pace. As a result, the Internet has become easy to access. Consequently, the number of Internet users has increased enormously. However, most users are not aware of how important it is to protect their data privacy on the Internet, especially as technology is constantly evolving. Moreover, users have encountered many types of threats that are associated with the Internet, but they may not be aware of them. Therefore, it is crucial to assess the risk associated with user behavior.
Most people seem to consider the Internet to be safe environment and use it on a daily basis using their smart phones, tablets and computers. It seems that they are unaware, or they do not know about that there are a large number of cyber-attacks that happen on every day. Interest in cyber security issues often focuses on incidents and how to deal with them after it had happened. While a concern for prevention in better cybersecurity have fallen behind.
This website is developed for the general internet users to access and increase their level of cybersecurity awareness.
