Cybersecurity Awareness: A Digital Media Approach

Phishing Scams

Phishing is a technique used by an attacker to steal private information from internet users by presenting themselves as trusted parties.

Man In The Middle Attack

This refers to an attack in which the attacker interferes with the communication flow from between two parties.

Phishing Attacks

Identity Theft

Since cyber laypersons are unaware of the risks and attempts used by attackers to phish, many of them would constitute a significant percentage of victims. Cyber laypersons can ignorantly click malicious links in emails or messages received from social media platforms. The malicious links can direct them to fake websites where sensitive information like credit card details could be collected and used to defraud the victims.

Man In The Middle Attacks

Cyber laypersons can fall victims to this type of cyber threat when they use their devices on unsecured Wi-Fi that has been compromised by a man-in-the-middle attack. The actors that execute such attacks usually take advantage of vulnerable networks to steal information sent across the network. Subsequently, the actors use the stolen data to cause harm either by attacking the person that the information was stolen from or on their contacts. Parading as a close relative increases the vulnerability of potential victims who will think they are communicating with a trusted and known person.
